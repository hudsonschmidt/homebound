## Description (4000 characters max)
Homebound is a personal safety app designed for outdoor adventurers. Whether you're hiking remote trails, climbing mountains, diving deep waters, or exploring the backcountry, Homebound ensures someone always knows where you are and when to expect you back.

**How It Works**

1. Create a Trip – Set your activity type, location, start time, and expected return time.
2. Add Emergency Contacts – Choose up to 3 contacts who will be notified if something goes wrong.
3. Check In – Tap to start your adventure when you head out.
4. Check Out – Mark yourself safe when you return. If you don't check out by your ETA plus a grace period, your contacts are automatically alerted with your trip details.

**Key Features**

• Activity-Specific Trips – Choose from 19 activity types including hiking, climbing, skiing, scuba diving, kayaking, and more. Each activity comes with tailored safety tips.

• Smart Notifications – Your emergency contacts receive detailed trip info including your location, activity type, notes, and expected return time.

• Flexible Grace Periods – Set a buffer time before overdue alerts trigger. Life happens—a few extra minutes won't cause a false alarm.

• Location Support – Add locations via search with map preview so your contacts know exactly where to find you.

• Trip History – View your past adventures and track your outdoor statistics.

• Offline Ready – Your trip details are cached locally so you can access them even without cell service.

• Privacy First – We don't track your location in the background. You control when to check in and out.

**Who Is Homebound For?**

• Solo hikers venturing into backcountry trails
• Climbers heading out for a day on the rocks
• Scuba divers who need surface interval accountability
• Skiers and snowboarders hitting the slopes
• Cyclists on long solo rides
• Kayakers and paddlers exploring waterways
• Anyone doing solo outdoor activities where safety matters

**Peace of Mind for You and Your Loved Ones**

A 10-second check-in can become a lifesaving signal. Homebound creates a simple safety net that gives you the freedom to explore while giving your loved ones peace of mind.

Download Homebound today and adventure with confidence.
